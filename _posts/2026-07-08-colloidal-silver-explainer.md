Colloidal silver comes up a lot in wellness conversations, and a lot of the information floating around about it is either overhyped or unclear. Here's a plain, careful explainer, with no exaggerated claims.

## What is colloidal silver?

Colloidal silver is a liquid containing very small silver particles suspended evenly throughout the liquid. It's a traditional wellness product that some people include as part of a general wellness routine. It is not a drug, and it is not intended to diagnose, cure, treat, or prevent any disease.

## What does "25 PPM" mean?

PPM stands for parts per million, and it describes the concentration of silver particles in the solution. A 25 PPM solution means there are 25 parts of silver for every million parts of liquid, a relatively dilute concentration. PPM is simply a measurement of concentration, not an indication of strength of effect.

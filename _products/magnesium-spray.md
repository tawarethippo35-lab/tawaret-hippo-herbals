Magnesium Spray is a transdermal magnesium chloride body spray applied directly to the skin for external use.

### Why customers choose it

- Included in the Colloidal Silver, Healing Oil and Magnesium Protocol for tingling sensations and nerve relaxation.
- May ease muscle fatigue and cramps.
- Supports circulation-focused body care and oxygen movement.
- Magnesium plays an important role in maintaining a normal heart rhythm.
- Many customers use it in the evening to help them settle down and prepare for better sleep. Research on magnesium for sleep is limited, so individual results vary.
- Can be sprayed under the feet, behind the knees, inside the elbows, or under the arms.
- Leaves no sticky residue.

Spray onto the desired area and allow it to absorb. Avoid the eyes and freshly shaved or broken skin. If tingling occurs, this may happen with topical magnesium use, especially for sensitive skin. A racing or irregular heartbeat requires medical attention and should not be managed with Magnesium Spray alone.
